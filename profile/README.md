## Overview

<a href="#">Infuse 8 Pro</a> plays video files that QuickTime and other players reject. The application handles virtually every video codec and container format through sophisticated decoding that leverages hardware acceleration when available. Whether playing MKV files with unusual audio codecs, high-bitrate 4K content, or legacy formats, Infuse delivers smooth playback.

The <a href="#">format support</a> spans decades of video technology. Modern codecs like HEVC and AV1 decode efficiently. Legacy formats from the DivX era play correctly. Audio codecs from AC3 to DTS to TrueHD process without external components. This comprehensive support eliminates codec hunting and transcoding workarounds.

<a href="#">Hardware decoding</a> enables smooth high-resolution playback. Apple Silicon handles 4K and 8K content efficiently. HDR video displays with proper tone mapping. Battery life extends on MacBooks through efficient decoding. Hardware acceleration transforms demanding content into effortless playback.

The <a href="#">library organization</a> brings order to video collections. Point Infuse at folders containing media. Automatic metadata fetching adds posters, synopses, and ratings. Browse visually through attractive grids. Library view transforms folder chaos into organized collections.

<a href="#">Server streaming</a> plays content from network sources. Connect to Plex, Emby, Jellyfin, and other media servers. Stream from NAS devices directly. Watch your library from anywhere on your network. Streaming extends Infuse beyond local files.

The <a href="#">playback controls</a> provide viewing comfort. Variable speed playback for quickly reviewing content. Chapter navigation jumps to specific segments. Audio track and subtitle selection for multi-language content. Controls address practical viewing needs.

<a href="#">Continue watching</a> tracks progress across devices. Stop on Mac, resume on Apple TV or iPhone. Sync maintains position automatically. Never lose your place in series or long films. Cross-device continuity enhances the viewing experience.
